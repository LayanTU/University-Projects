Healthy Lifestyle Tips

  • Eat balanced meals: fruits, vegetables, protein, and whole grains.
  • Exercise daily: walk, run, or simple workouts.
  • Get enough sleep: 7-9 hours for energy and mood.
  • Drink water regularly: 2-3 liters daily.
  • Avoid stress: relax, breathe deeply, and take short breaks.
